# Design Task List

Ten improvement ideas for the next iteration of Pawsitive Detectives (also shown in-game under the ✅ Task List tab):

1. Pet personalities that matter: movement and clues should reflect the pet personality.
2. Missing item cases: add stolen toys, buried trophies and lost pet accessories.
3. Detective notebook: allow players to record theories and predictions.
4. Rescue celebrations: create thank-you letters, happy owner moments and pet photos.
5. Seasonal events: add summer, winter, Halloween and holiday pet cases.
6. Pet HQ display room: show cards, photos, trophies and legendary rescue displays.
7. Newspaper reports: publish cute headlines after major rescues.
8. Pet show competitions: bring rescued pets back as community event characters.
9. Witness relationship system: recurring NPCs give better clues over time.
10. Legendary case board: long multi-part mysteries such as Phantom Pawprints.
